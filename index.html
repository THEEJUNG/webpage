I am an applied scientist at Amazon Alexa AI. Before that, I received a Ph.D in Statistics at Univeristy of Pittsburgh, a master in Statistics at University of California Berkeley, and a Bachelor in Management Science at KAIST. Prior to the graduate study, I worked as a data scientist at Korea Credit Bureau (KCB) for 5+ years. My main role was to develop predictive and statistical models, and consult clients in financial industry and governments. My main research interest is to quantify the uncertainty in context of Natural Language Processing (NLP). I am also broadly interested in ML/DL applications on NLP problems.
